Functionalities of the Website

Welcome to our official website, an open-data platform designed to provide a more user-friendly way to access ancient Fraktur minutes. Our platform offers easy and convenient access to valuable historical documents in Fraktur script and modern German scripts, making it easier for users to explore and research the past

· Step 1: Go to the Datasets page.

· Step 2: You can use the search box on Datasets page to filter the data.

· Step 3: The highlight box on the Data page enables you to quickly locate the location of your keywords within a vast amount of text, saving you time and effort.

· Step 4: If you want to check the source page, you can find it under the Resource Documents according to the page number.

· Step 5: Should you have any questions or concerns regarding the content of our data, please don't hesitate to reach out to the maintainer whose contact information can be found at the bottom of the page. Our team is committed to ensuring the accuracy and integrity of the information provided, and we welcome any feedback that can help us improve our platform.

How to upload a new page

To maintain the quality and reliability of our platform, only authorized personnel or individuals with explicit permission are allowed to upload new data. This policy is in place to ensure that all information on our website is accurate and relevant, while also preventing the introduction of any unnecessary or potentially problematic content. Thank you for your understanding and cooperation in helping us maintain the integrity of our data.

If you have administrative privileges, please navigate to Github page and review the README file.

If you do not have administrative privileges, please visit the Help page and reach out to the administrator to obtain the necessary permissions.
